Quick Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I create an event?

To create an event, sign in as an organizer, navigate to your dashboard, and click "Create Event." Follow the prompts to add details, set pricing, and publish your event.

How do refunds work?

Refund policies are set by event organizers. You can find the specific refund policy for each event on its details page. Contact the organizer directly for refund requests.

Where are my tickets?

After purchasing tickets, you can find them in your "My Tickets" section. You'll also receive a confirmation email with ticket details and QR codes.

How do I become an organizer?

To become an event organizer, click on "Register as Organizer" in the navigation menu. Complete the registration form with your organization details to get started.
